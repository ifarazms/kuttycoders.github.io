ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

Kuttycoders is a new venture for children with a passion for technology to learn new things and meet new people thereby immersing themselves in a growing community of doers. Technology can be used to learn many different things, from how the world works to new ways to solve a specific problem in our world today and in the future.
